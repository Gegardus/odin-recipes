Tatar-Buragi

Description

Tatar Buragi is one of my favorite traditional dishes of Armenia. It is at the same time unpretentious and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Matsun (Natural Yoghurt)
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Knead unleavened dough in the desired quantity.
  2. Roll it out thinly and cut into diamonds or squares of any size so that you can eat it at a time.
  3. Place the diamonds on a floured surface to dry slightly.
  4. At this time, boil water, add salt as for pasta.
  5. Place tatar buragi in water and cook until tender (usually 6-8 minutes).
  6. When ready, drain the water, put on plates, pour over melted butter (about 2-3 tablespoons per plate) and a mixture of matsun and crushed garlic in the desired amount.
